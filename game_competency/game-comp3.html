Competency-based interview

In a competency-based interview the interviewer will ask you to talk about times and situations in your day to day life when youâ€™ve actually used the skills they are looking for. Think about some examples of these and use the STARR strategy to help you prepare.

  • Situation: Start by setting the scene, describe the situation.
  • Task: Describe what you had to do.
  • Action: Describe how you set about completing the task.
  • Result: Describe the outcome and how it went, e.g was it successful or were there any difficulties, including any feedback you received.
  • Reflection: Describe what you learned during this experience and what you might do differently next time.
