Modern Apprentice

Vacancy: Modern Apprenticeship (Accounting)
Employer: DIY Giant
Hours: Full time (35 hours a week)
Salary: £4.67 an hour
Description:You will assist the finance department with processing invoices, processing purchase ledger payment runs, dealing with any supplier queries, reconciling supplier statements, and assisting the payroll department with timesheet processing. You will work with a team of professionals, learning on the job and attending college one day per week. You will get full induction, training and support from the company and our training partners to help you achieve the Accountancy qualification at SCQF Level 6. You should have some knowledge and experience of accounting/finance work. Skills preferred include communication, numeracy, IT literacy and organisation. You should be able to work as part of a team, work to deadlines and produce accurate work. There is expected to be strong competition for this role, so we will be conducting a screening interview in the first instance.
